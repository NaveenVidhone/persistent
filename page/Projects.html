1.Proximity Sensor Car

  • Proximity sensor car with object detecting based on IR receiver and transmitter. A proximity sensor often emits an electromagnetic field or a beam of electromagnetic radiation (infrared, for instance), and looks for changes in the field or return signal.
  • We used the different IC's like L293D,7805,LM358 and many different components.
  • It was designed and stimulated in Eagle and implemented on PCB.

2.Laser Security System

  • The basic sensing component of a modern laser security system is an infrared motion detector. An infrared motion detector works by using beams of infrared light to detect changes in heat. When a person moves into the beam of light, the sensor is alerted by the body heat given off by the individual. Which works on the principle of light emitting diode.
  • It was designed and stimulated in Eagle and implemented on PCB.
